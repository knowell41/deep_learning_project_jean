Introduction

Detecting engagement among students in online classes using facial recognition. The dataset used considered pictures of students taken using laptop cameras during online classes and their various facial emotions.

How does it work ?

1. At the navigation bar, click the App tab.

2. You will see a file upload form. Select and browse target photo to classify.

3. Click submit button. The selected photo will be evaluated by the model as "Engaged" or "Not Engaged".

4. You can upload new photo to evaluate.
